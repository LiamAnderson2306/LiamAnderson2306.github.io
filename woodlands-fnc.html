Our story

The local chippy, done properly.

Tucked into Woodlands Village, we've been frying up fresh Western Australian fish for the neighbourhood for years — crispy golden batter, hand-cut chips and that proper fish-and-chip-shop feel.

Grilled or fried, a quick feed or a family pack — it's all cooked fresh when you order. No shortcuts, just good honest fish and chips.
