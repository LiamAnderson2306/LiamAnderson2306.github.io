The House

A romantic hideaway in the hills.

Set in a sensitively restored 110-year-old home, Two Sisters & A Chef is all open fireplaces, antiques and quiet corners — five dining rooms and an enclosed veranda made for special evenings.

Seasonal, considered cooking led by head chef Scott Jerkovich: house-made pasta, market seafood and beautifully cooked steaks, matched to a thoughtful cellar.
