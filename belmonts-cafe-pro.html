Our story

More than a café — it's where the street starts its day.

For years Belmont's has been the corner you come back to: the first coffee before work, the long weekend brunch, the catch-up that runs three cups deep. Real food, made fresh, served by people who know your order.

We roast local, cook from scratch, and keep the welcome warm — seven days a week.
